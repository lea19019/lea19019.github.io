Fitt's Law

Brilliant

brilliant.org

Brilliant.org is a perfect example of the Fitt's Law. As you can see on the screenshot above it has some buttons which are easily recognizable. Brilliant has it clear what they want from us, and makes it easy for us to click on it. This webpage also has some very nice features I cover in the other post, Brilliant has a excellent webpage.

Hick's Law

Mercado Libre

mercadolibre.com

Mercado Libre is a Latin American webpage for buying online, it is very similar to Amazon. As with many online shops it has many options from where to choose, this is why it's an good example of the Hick's Law. Mercado Libre knowing that with too many choices people mostlikely won't end up buying something they included categories in their webpage.

White Space

Disney

disney.com

Disney is known for being such a good designer, their webpage is not the exception. The White Space principle is very well applied in their webpage, we can see how the white background has an excellent contrast with the content on the page. Even thou it has a lot of appelaing content, it all fits perfectly to allow the white also have an important part and make it look clean.
