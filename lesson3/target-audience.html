Target Audience

The target audience for this website is the people that enjoy to take walks, either young, middle age and not so young.

Two personas

Persona 1: Young and unexperience people that don't know much about walks. Age within 16-30 years, lots of energy and a great desire to explore the nature. Looking for tips and information to have a better experience in walks.
Persona 2: Mature people that wants to know if certain day is going to be a good idea to take a walk. Usually with in 30 or more years. Cautios people.

Scenarios

One: I want to know if it's a good idea to go to run this afternoon.
Two: I would like to buy a nice sweater for my morning runs, which should I choose?
Three: My family and me are planning to have a picnic this weekend, is it a good idea?
Four: Is it dangerous to take a walk in the forest after rain?
Five: I want to take a walk, what are somethings I should think about?
